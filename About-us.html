About Us

The IEEE Student Branch at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra brings together a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts committed to engineering excellence and innovation.

We organize hands-on workshops, seminars, and hackathons to help students grow beyond textbooks. Our goal is to inspire future engineers, empower creativity, and connect students with global technological advances while staying rooted in humanitarian values.
